# Aid Map

## Smart Resource Allocation – Data-Driven Volunteer Coordination for Social Impact

Aid Map is a smart web-based platform designed to connect NGOs, volunteers, and communities efficiently. It helps NGOs identify urgent community needs, prioritize them using AI, and assign the right volunteers to the right tasks based on skills, availability, and location.

The platform improves transparency, faster response, and better social impact by centralizing scattered NGO data into one accessible system.

---

## Problem Statement

Many NGOs collect important community needs data through surveys, field visits, and reports, but this information often remains scattered and difficult to manage.

Because of this:

- Urgent needs are delayed
- Volunteers are not assigned efficiently
- Resources are wasted
- Communities do not receive timely support

Aid Map solves this problem using AI-powered prioritization and smart volunteer coordination.
